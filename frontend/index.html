Start a Vibe in 3 Simple Steps

1. Create a Room

Sign in and create a new Vibe room. Give it a name and choose a mood, like 'OPM Throwback' or 'Pang-Sawi'.

2. Add Your Music

Paste any YouTube video or playlist link to add songs to the queue. Everyone in the room can suggest tracks.

3. Listen Together

Share the room link with friends. Everyone hears the same music at the exact same time, no matter where they are.
